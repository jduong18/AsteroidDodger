Asteroid Dodger

Asteroid Dodger is simple dodging game that becomes harder as time goes on.
Control your ship as asteroids fly across the screen.

How to Play:
Use WASD to move your rocket across the screen
Be careful as you cannot move diagonally
Asteroids will move faster as time moves on
If you can survive for 40 seconds, you win!
