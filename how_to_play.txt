After installing the game (check READ_ME), simply go into the 'src' file and run dodging-game.exe (dodging-game Application) to load up the game.


Press space to start the game and once the game starts, just use WASD to move the rocket ship around and dodge any asteroids coming 
your way, survive for 40 seconds and you win!. If you beat the game or get hit, just press space to restart.
